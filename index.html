About

About the Business

Vintage Toys and Collectables Aberdeen is based in Aberdeen city centre. We buy and sell a wide range of vintage toys and collectibles.

  • We stock many classic 80s & 90s toylines from Masters of the Universe, Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles, Stars Wars, Power Rangers and Star Trek to more modern collectables like Funko Pops, Pokémon, Marvel legends and many others.
  • We have one of the largest Collections of Funko Pops! available in the city covering many different genres form Marvel, Anime, Disney, DC, gaming.
  • Our Pokémon card wall holds 240 cards with thousands more available in our Binders

Looking to sell your old collection? We offer both buy and trade in options. Our stock is updated daily so make sure you keep popping back to se what else we have in stock our check out our Facebook Page to see the latest arrivals.
